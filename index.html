Our Journey

Andrew and Christina went on their first date at Tommy’s Mexican Restaurant on June 30, 2014. Anxious and getting over a cold that left him coughing and constantly apologizing for it, he didn’t expect a second date. However, they kept talking and Andrew planned a very romantic second date, an outing to see Transformers: Age of Extinction. She didn’t like the movie but she enjoyed her company. What followed were 8 years of laughter, fun, and growing up together.

Andrew and Christina have given love and support as the other went through tough times; they also challenged each other to reach greater happiness and success. They are very excited for you to join them at their wedding as they take their next steps together as a married couple.
